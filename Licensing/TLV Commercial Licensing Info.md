## Why Commercial Licensing?

The TriLateral Voice (TLV) Framework is **free for non-commercial use** under Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 International (CC BY-NC-SA 4.0) license. This enables individuals, educators, non-profits, and government agencies to use TLV without cost or permission.

However, **for-profit commercial use requires a commercial license** from The TLV Foundation Series. Commercial licensing serves multiple purposes:

‚úÖ **Supports TLV Development**: License fees fund ongoing framework development, research, documentation, and community resources

‚úÖ **Ensures Quality**: Commercial licensees meet quality standards and receive official recognition and support

‚úÖ **Protects the Brand**: Licensing prevents misuse and ensures TLV name represents integrity and mission-alignment

‚úÖ **Enables Free Access**: Revenue from commercial licenses keeps TLV free for mission-aligned non-commercial uses

‚úÖ **Provides Support**: Commercial licensees receive professional support, updates, and resources

## Licensing Options and Pricing

The TLV Foundation offers flexible licensing to accommodate different business models and scales.

### **Option 1: Annual License + Revenue Share**

**Best for:** Established service providers with ongoing TLV-based revenue

**Structure:**
- **Annual License Fee:** $5,000 - $15,000 (based on organization size and scope)
- **Revenue Share:** 5-15% of gross revenue from TLV-based services
- **Minimum Annual Payment:** Annual license fee serves as minimum

**Benefits:**
- Predictable base cost
- Scales with your success
- Full commercial rights to TLV Framework and Marks

**Example:**
- Small consulting firm: $5,000/year + 8% revenue share
- Medium training company: $10,000/year + 10% revenue share
- Large enterprise: $15,000/year + 12% revenue share

---

### **Option 2: Tiered Revenue Share**

**Best for:** Startups and growth-stage businesses

**Structure:**
- **No upfront annual fee**
- **Tiered percentage based on annual gross revenue from TLV services:**
  - $0 - $50,000: 5%
  - $50,001 - $250,000: 10%
  - $250,001 - $1,000,000: 15%
  - Over $1,000,000: 20%

**Benefits:**
- No initial cost barrier
- Low cost when starting out
- Scales as you grow

---

### **Option 3: Per-Use/Per-Client Licensing**

**Best for:** Occasional commercial use or pilot programs

**Structure:**
- **Per Client Project:** $250 - $1,000 per project (based on project scope)
- **Training Programs:** $25 - $100 per participant
- **Software Licensing:** $10 - $50 per seat/month

**Benefits:**
- Pay only when you use TLV commercially
- Ideal for testing TLV in your practice
- No ongoing commitment

---

### **Option 4: Enterprise/Institutional License**

**Best for:** Large organizations implementing TLV across departments

**Structure:**
- **Custom negotiated terms**
- **Unlimited internal use**
- **Dedicated support and customization**
- **Organization-wide training and certification**

**Contact us** for enterprise pricing and custom arrangements.

---

### **Option 5: Academic/Small Non-Profit Commercial License**

**Best for:** Universities with fee-based programs or small non-profits with earned income activities

**Structure:**
- **Reduced rate:** $1,000 - $3,000/year
- **Limited revenue share:** 3-5%
- **Must meet mission-alignment criteria**

**Requirements:**
- 501(c)(3) or educational institution status
- Primary mission aligns with TLV values
- Limited commercial activity (under $100K annual revenue from TLV)

After execution:

- Access to commercial licensee resources
- Brand assets and marketing materials
- Listing on Foundation website
- Ongoing support begins

**Timeline:** Typically 4-8 weeks from inquiry to executed agreement

## Frequently Asked Questions

### **Can I try TLV before committing to a commercial license?**

Yes! You can:
- Use TLV for personal learning and development (free)
- Implement TLV in a non-profit context (free)
- Do limited pilot testing with pro bono clients
- Attend public TLV training or workshops

When you're ready to offer commercial services, apply for a license.

### **What if I'm already using TLV commercially without a license?**

Contact us immediately to regularize your situation. We're happy to work with you to:
- Understand your current use
- Determine appropriate licensing
- Develop reasonable transition plan
- Grandfather existing client commitments

We prioritize education over enforcement, but ongoing unlicensed commercial use may result in legal action.

### **Can I sublicense TLV to my clients or partners?**

No. Your license is non-transferable and non-sublicensable. Your clients receive services from you; they don't receive independent rights to TLV marks.

If your clients want to implement TLV themselves commercially, they need their own license.

### **What if TLV Framework changes or updates?**

Foundation will:
- Provide advance notice of major changes when possible
- Offer support for transitioning to updated protocols
- Not make changes designed specifically to harm commercial licensees
- Consult with commercial licensee community on significant updates

You're encouraged to contribute feedback and improvements based on your commercial experience.

### **Can I modify TLV protocols for my clients?**

Yes, within limits:
- You can adapt implementation for specific client contexts
- You can create proprietary tools and systems that use TLV
- You can develop training materials and curricula
- You must accurately represent core TLV methodology
- Significant modifications should be disclosed and may require approval

### **What happens if my business grows significantly?**

Great! For revenue share models:
- Percentages increase with revenue tiers automatically
- Higher revenue = more support to Foundation mission

For fixed fee models:
- We may discuss tier adjustment at renewal
- We'll work with you to ensure fee remains fair and reasonable

### **Do you offer payment plans?**

For annual fees: Yes, quarterly installments available

For revenue share: Already structured as quarterly payments

For startups/small businesses: We'll work with you on reasonable arrangements

### **What about international licensing?**

Yes, we license worldwide. Terms are the same but:
- Consider currency exchange for USD payments
- May need to address international tax/legal considerations
- Same quality standards apply globally

### **Can non-profits get commercial licenses?**

Non-profits with earned income activities that use TLV commercially should get a commercial license for those activities.

However:
- Reduced rates available (Academic/Small Non-Profit option)
- Mission-aligned non-profits get favorable terms
- Core mission work (non-commercial) remains free

### **How do I prove revenue for revenue share reporting?**

Quarterly reports should include:
- Total gross revenue from TLV-based services
- Breakdown by service type
- Good faith certification by authorized officer

Foundation may audit once annually if concerns arise. Most licensees never experience audit‚Äîwe operate on trust and good faith.
